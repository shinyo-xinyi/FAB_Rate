Program Wizard
1. In the Amazon product page, point the mouse to the product(name) to be read.

2. The "Score" is calculated according to the comments of the product. The higher the score, the better the comments.

3. Click the "+Contrast" button, add this item to the comparison list.

4. Check the comparison list at the right-top corner.

5. "Sort" function helps to find the product with the best score.

6. Click the product box to navigate to the location of this product in the page.

7. Click the "minimize" button to hide the window.

The software wizard is over. Have a good time.
